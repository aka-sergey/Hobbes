# Document Shapes For `comms`

Use these patterns when polishing a raw draft into its final form.

## Telegram update

- one short paragraph or a few compact bullets
- keep the decision and the main risk visible

## Proposal note

- short framing
- offer or recommendation
- next step

## Memo

- topic
- what matters now
- action or decision

## Checklist

- short lead line
- ordered items

## Brief

- objective
- context
- key points
- next step
