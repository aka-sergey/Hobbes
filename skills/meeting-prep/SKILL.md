# Meeting Prep

Use this skill before meetings and calls.

## What it is for

- meeting briefs
- agendas
- attendee summaries
- talking points
- post-meeting action lists

## Core workflow

1. Identify:
   - meeting goal
   - participants
   - context
   - desired outcome
2. Prepare a compact brief.
3. Turn the brief into an agenda or call plan.
4. After the meeting, convert notes into next steps.

## Output pattern

- `brief`
- `agenda`
- `key_talking_points`
- `next_steps`

## Guardrails

- prefer operational clarity over generic business language
- do not invent attendee facts
- keep the agenda short enough to use live on a call
